## Event Types

### Limited-Time Events (2 weeks each)

**Type 1 - Challenge Events:**
- Complete 50 objectives
- Unlock tier-1 free cosmetic
- Unlock tier-2 premium cosmetic

**Type 2 - Tournament Events:**
- Ranked competition
- Top 100 get cosmetics
- Prizes: $1,000-5,000

**Type 3 - Story Events:**
- Campaign missions
- Unlock character skins
- Narrative progression

**Type 4 - Celebration Events:**
- Holiday events (Xmas, Halloween)
- Themed cosmetics
- Limited skins (no re-release)
